SPEEDTEST VPN™

Speedtest VPN ensures your online privacy and security from the convenience of your Speedtest® mobile application. Get started securing your connection with 2 GB of free data over VPN per month or upgrade to Premium for unlimited data protection.

Security you can trust

Speedtest VPN safeguards your online identity by encrypting your device’s internet connection and
anonymizing your IP address and location. When enabled, Speedtest VPN routes your internet
connection through a trusted server so you can use the web and apps from your mobile device
with complete confidence in your digital security.

With Speedtest VPN, you can
  • Defend yourself against potential privacy breaches on both private and public networks.
  • Maintain the privacy of messages you send and receive online.
  • Prevent third parties from accessing or selling your information to advertisers and other groups.
  • Access content from anywhere without restrictions.
  • Stay protected in every context and location that you use your phone.
